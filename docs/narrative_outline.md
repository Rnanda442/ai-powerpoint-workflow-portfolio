# Narrative Outline

## Working Title

AI as a Research Operating System

## Core Thesis

The story is not just "using ChatGPT." It is a workflow for turning messy source
material, code, screenshots, GIS data, notebooks, and conversations into reusable
research infrastructure: dashboards, manuscripts, visualizations, and
presentation-ready outputs.

## Section 1: The Workflow Loop

Start with the repeatable loop:

1. Ask and prototype in ChatGPT/Codex.
2. Capture durable notes in Obsidian or Markdown.
3. Bring in source documents, PDFs, GIS files, notebooks, screenshots, and code.
4. Accumulate hundreds of pages into searchable working context.
5. Extract actionable insights and convert them into maps, dashboards, papers,
   slides, or decisions.

Visual candidates:

- Chat/workflow screenshots from contact sheet 07.
- Document and calendar screenshots from contact sheet 07.
- Source library and manuscript draft files from the gas hydrates project.
- Google Drive documents such as `pdf combined`, `PDF notes reorganized`, and
  `new query with all stuff`.

## Section 3: Streamlit Websites Built With AI

Use two concrete examples:

- Stock research dashboard: automated pipeline, GitHub Actions, model baselines,
  ranked watchlists, local database export, and 3D stock universe.
- North Slope gas hydrate dashboard: GIS atlas, structural explorer, data
  library, research framework, and synthetic well-log planning scaffold.

These are strong proof that AI is being used to build working software, not just
write notes.

## Section 4.5: Notebook As App Prototype

The earthquake project appears to live as a Jupyter workflow rather than a
Processing/p5 app. It can still become a Streamlit section because it already
has app-like stages:

1. Search earthquake catalogs.
2. Match stations and channels.
3. Download MiniSEED waveform traces.
4. Pick P-wave arrivals.
5. Calculate distance and apparent velocity.
6. Map events and station geometry.
7. Estimate unknown/water segment velocity from path segmentation.

## Section 5: From Sources To Paper

Show the research-writing architecture:

The key message: AI helps compress the distance between reading, synthesis,
analysis, and communication.

## Section 6: What Gets Streamlined

Frame the impact:

- finding relevant material faster
- preserving reasoning between sessions
- converting screenshots and notes into structured evidence
- moving from exploration to draft to dashboard to slides
- making research workflows reproducible
